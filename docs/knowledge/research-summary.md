## Priority-Ordered Ecosystem Map

Based on Scott Power's stated priorities and current activity levels:

### Tier 1 — Flagship Initiatives (This Year's Focus)

| # | Initiative | Why Top Priority |
|---|-----------|-----------------|
| 1 | **NOT REAL ART** | Arterial's most mature and active program. 200 podcast episodes, 30+ First Friday exhibitions, 36 grant recipients, 175+ Q+ART profiles, Remote video series, NRA School, and now a full AI-powered submission pipeline (artwork-archive). |
| 2 | **Arthouse** | This year's biggest new initiative. Unscripted TV series with HGTV veteran showrunner (Stacy Schneider, 300+ episodes). Chicago pilot filmed. $150K Indiegogo campaign. Targeting Netflix/Hulu/Apple TV. |

### Tier 2 — Major Active Programs

| # | Initiative | Why This Tier |
|---|-----------|--------------|
| 3 | **First Friday Exhibition Series** | Deserves separate mention (not just a sub-bullet of NRA). 30+ curated online exhibitions since 2023, powered by the artwork-archive AI pipeline. Monthly cadence with professional curation by Morgan Laurens. |
| 4 | **Artsville USA** | Next up on priority and recency. "Tale of Two Cities" exhibition at NOAFA (40+ artists, hurricane recovery narrative). Louise Glickman Grant & Residency ($10K) launching 2026. New Executive Director. Acquired as Arterial subsidiary. |
| 5 | **Classic Black** | Steinway Artist Ric'key Pageot (Madonna's touring pianist). Sold-out concerts (Place des Arts Montreal). Documentary in development. Strong impact narrative around Black excellence in classical music. |

### Tier 3 — Distribution & Partnerships

| # | Initiative | Why This Tier |
|---|-----------|--------------|
| 6 | **Artbound / PBS** | Emmy Award-winning series (5 Emmys in 2024, 2 more in 2025). Season 15. Arterial is financial sponsor. PBS SoCal partnership. |
| 7 | **The Underbelly Project** | $12K Arterial grant. Documentary about secret subway art installation (100+ artists). Premiered Miami Art Week Dec 2024. |
| 8 | **Random Media** | LA-based indie distributor — Arterial's path to streaming platforms. |

### Tier 4 — In Development

|---|-----------|--------------|

| 10 | **Arterial Radio** | Internet-based arts talk radio. In development, no launch date yet. |
| 11 | **Ready To Roll** | Documentary about concert roadies. In development at Crewest. (Not previously listed in project docs.) |
